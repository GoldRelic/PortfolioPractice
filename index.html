I am Tomohiro Nishikado, i'm a Japanese video game developer and engineer. I am the creator of the arcade shoot 'em up game Space Invaders, often credited as the first shoot 'em up, i also designed other earlier Taito arcade games, including the shooting electro-mechanical games Sky Fighter and Sky Fighter II, the sports video game TV Basketball, the vertical scrolling racing video game Speed Race, the multi-directional shooter Western Gun, and the first-person combat flight simulator Interceptor.
